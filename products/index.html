Flowster Platform

Transform natural language into intelligent automation workflows

Describe your desired workflow in plain English (or any other language), and Flowster automatically creates executable automation flows that integrate with your existing tools and services. Free your business from big-tech's flavor of the week; build adaptive solutions as unique as your team - no developers needed!
Platform Capabilities

Natural Language Processing

Describe your automation needs in plain English. Our AI understands your intent and converts it into executable workflows.

Modular Execution

Each workflow node is a native Flowster module that executes as a Lambda endpoint, ensuring reliability and scalability.

Seamless Integrations

Connect to Google Workspace, Microsoft Office, databases, APIs, and hundreds of other services through our extensive node library.

Dynamic Workflow Engine

Workflows adapt and scale automatically based on your data and business logic, with built-in error handling, recursive learning, and retry mechanisms.

How Flowster Works
1

Describe Your Workflow

Tell Flowster what you want to automate using natural language. No technical expertise required.

2

AI Translation

Our NLP engine captures your intent and converts it into our Domain Specific Language (DSL) with proper data and process dependencies.

3

Code Generation

The DSL is automatically converted into native Flowster scripts that call our Lambda-hosted MCP node objects.

4

Execution & Monitoring

Your workflow runs on our secure cloud infrastructure with real-time monitoring and automatic scaling.
